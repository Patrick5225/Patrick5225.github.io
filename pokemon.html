Pokémon Go: Case Study in Mathematical Modeling

Third-party models for Pokémon Go are well distributed but fundamentally flawed. Currently, the most accurate predictions are made by full simulations which disagree with the popular, third-party models. This project aims to provide more accurate mathematical models and heuristics. The results in this project find agreement with simulations, where other models have not.

Comparing Results with Third-Party Results

Third-Party Best Attack Moveset for Raichu

The best move set for Raichu according to Eurogamer (as of June 11, 2017) is Volt Switch, Wild Charge because the sum of damage per second (dps) of volt switch and dps of wild charge has the largest value sum for a fast move and charged move pair.

Note: After this project was created, the original article from Eurogamer seems to no longer exist, and instead redirects to a different article from the same author.

Original link to the article: https://www.eurogamer.net/articles/2017-11-06-pokemon-go-moves-movesets-move-list-and-highest-dps-attacks (redirects to a different article from December, 2018)

Dps was calculated by dividing power by duration of move.

Name Type Basic Attack Charge Attack
Raichu Electric Volt Switch (8.7) Brick Break (25)
Spark (8.57) Thunder Punch (25)
Wild Charge (34.62)

However, there are flaws with these results:

  1. Dps calculation: Does not consider significance of STAB multipliers in calculation
  2. Sum of individual dps fast + dps charged: does not consider paired relationship.

Compared to our best move set calculated for Raichu: Spark, Wild Charge

Name Type Basic Attack Charge Attack Total DPS for Moveset
Raichu Electric Spark Wild Charge 22.80822
Volt Switch Wild Charge 21.66667
Spark Thunder Punch 18.41221
Volt Switch Thunder Punch 17.71712
Spark Brick Break 15.936
Volt Switch Brick break 15.4616

Differences being that:

  1. Calculated dps in consideration of STAB multipliers
  2. Took in account of paired relationship of fast move, and charged move in dps, rather than taking sum of the individual dps.

Other models such as from Pokemon Go Info inaccurately claims that Volt Switch, Wild Charge is the best move set for Raichu because the sum of dps for volt switch and wild charge has the largest value sum.
