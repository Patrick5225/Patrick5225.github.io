Pokémon Go: Case Study in Mathematical Modeling

Third-party models for Pokémon Go are well distributed but fundamentally flawed. Currently, the most accurate predictions are made by full simulations which disagree with the popular, third-party models. This project provides more accurate mathematical models and heuristics. The results find agreement with simulations, where other models have not.
